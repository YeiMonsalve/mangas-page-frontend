Mangas

Here you can find all our available manga. If you're looking for a specific genre, you can go to the catalog located at the top of the page.

Another

For 26 years, the third class of the ninth grade at Yomiyama North Middle School has been plagued by a terrible curse. In 1972, a popular student named Misaki died in a tragic accident. Since then, strange death-related events have haunted the students of that class.

Attack on Titan

The story takes place in a fictional world in which humanity is on the brink of extinction due to humanoid creatures called "titans", forcing the survivors to take refuge in three enormous walls that prevent access to these monsters.

Bunny girl senpai

High school student Sakuta Azusagawa's life takes an unexpected turn when he meets teenage actress Mai Sakurajima, dressed as a bunny girl, wandering around a library and no one else notices. Mai is intrigued because Sakuta is the only one who can see her, since other people can't see her, even when she dresses normally or tries to stay away from the celebrity life. Calling this phenomenon "Adolescence Syndrome"

Corpse Party

Based on the video game of the same name, Corpse Party: Tortured Souls follows the main story of five students who find themselves transported to a mysterious institute inhabited by numerous ghosts who want to end their lives.

Death Note

Light Yagami, a dedicated model student, is extremely bored. But when he finds a mysterious notebook, infinite possibilities open up before him: with the power of life or death of his fellow men in his hands, which path will he choose? Help or create a world where he is the overlord?

Fruits Basket

It tells the story of young Tooru, an orphan student who lives in a tent. Tooru's strong and determined character makes him live day to day with resolution.

Hell Girl

It tells the story of a little girl who is abandoned by her parents for being born with malformations, the girl tries to return to them.

Jujutsu Kaisen

The story revolves around student Yūji Itadori, who joins a secret organization of sorcerers to kill a powerful curse called Ryomen Sukuna after becoming its host.

Lovely Complex

This shojo manga is a love story different from all the others you know; a story about why differences are important, but also about the willpower necessary to overcome complexes.

Naruto

The work tells the story of a teenage ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, who aspires to become Hokage, leader of his village, with the purpose of being recognized as someone important within the village and among his peers. Discover these great adventures in their manga version.

Neon Genesis Evangelion

Evangelion is a 'robot' series where the enemy robots are the least important aspect, yet they drive the plot forward. Evangelion blends action scenes with high-caliber, cryptic dialogues, invoking God and the right to create a new Humanity.

Yakuza Lover

When a feisty college girl falls for a yakuza boss, she gets swept into a steamy and dangerous love affair.
